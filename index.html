ADEKUNLE GOLD

ADEKUNLE KOSOKO AKA Adekunle Gold recently recognized as AG BABY

He is a Nigerian Afro pop singer who was born on the 28th of january 1987 to the Kosoko Family in Lagos State

Adekunle studied visual arts in the Lagos State Polytechnic, after graduation he started creating designs for people and artist , he created the famous YBNL logo and numerous album arts for YBNL acts
Adekunle Gold was part of a band called the bridge which consisted of him and his best friend Seyifunmi, they had a collaboration with EME star act skales, oe of their popular works was the cover of flavours "ADA" called "Omolade" He came into the spotlight in 2015 with the hit single "Sade" a highlife cover of the One Direction's "Story of my Life" , The success of that single paved the way for him to be signed to YBNL record Label , Adekunle Gold also released hit songs like Orente which was reportedly recorded for his wife and Pick up
He has 2 studio albums to his name namely Gold and About 30 , Gold was released in 2015 , he Left YBNL the following year repotedly due to a clause in his contract. A New album is expectred to drop on he 21st of August , Named Afro Pop , It is highly anticipated because of the success behind the singles released off the album

Adekunle Gold also got crowned the king of photoshop by nigerian social media uses due to his exceptional photoshop skills ,which he displayed by putting himself in oictures with celebrities like Tiwa Savage ,boy band group One direction,Empire e.t.c in 2015.

Adekunle is Married to superstar female Artist Simisola Kosoko , popularly known as Simi, their marriage is blessed with a baby
